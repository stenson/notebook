Jia!

Teoswa food across the world

Welcome! Have you eaten yet? Please, take a seat at our Teoswa table.

We’re Diana & Rob, and this is our (work-in-progress) hub for all things related to Jia!, a cookbook project documenting the food of the Teoswa diaspora. This project was successfully funded via Kickstarter in October 2016 — thank you, wonderful supporters! We’re headed to Thailand, Vietnam, and China on a research trip in January 2017 and will be sharing updates from the road here and on our respective Instagram and Twitter accounts.

If you would like to support our project, you can purchase a tote bag or fridge magnet through our shop. You can also place a pre-order for our (digital or print) book there!

Until next time, happy eating!
